Not just a race track

The home of the Dutch Grand Prix is more than just another racetrack. How do you show that versatility to the various target groups? To the racing fan looking for a spectacular 'day out at in Zandvoort', but also to the event manager curious about the business opportunities and packages. We took up this challenge by mapping and redesigning all information flows together with various stakeholders. With a fresh and intuitive UI design and a powerful Craft engine under the hood, this platform is ready for the future and Circuit Zandvoort's ambitions.
