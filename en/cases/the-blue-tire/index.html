Make the blue tire world famous

That was the briefing. We developed a campaign for Swapfiets, sponsor of Team Jumbo-Visma, which caught the eye of marketers and cycling enthusiasts everywhere. We made the blue tire part of the world's biggest cycling race, the Tour de France. Set a world record for the largest banner ever, one that was seen live on TV by millions of viewers worldwide. And inspired the Dutch YouTube channel Tour de Tietema to climb Mont Ventoux on a Swapfiets, while reaching the desired target groups through a sophisticated (social) media strategy. The Blue Tire campaign was much admired and widely rewarded.
